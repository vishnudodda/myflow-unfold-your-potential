# MyFlow Reasoning Engine — Career Discovery

> Each question is mapped to primary/secondary traits, per-option weighted contributions, higher-level dimensions, framework relevance, confidence logic, and downstream contributions.

## New traits introduced

- **Extrinsic Motivation** (`extrinsic_motivation`) — Driven by external rewards such as compensation, status, and formal recognition.
- **Intrinsic Motivation** (`intrinsic_motivation`) — Driven by internal rewards, personal interest, and inherent task satisfaction.
- **Autonomy Preference** (`autonomy`) — Desire to be self-directed and have strong control over one's own work and choices.
- **Purpose Orientation** (`purpose_orientation`) — Motivated by a desire to contribute to something larger than oneself and make a societal impact.
- **Structural Preference** (`structural_preference`) — Seeks defined, organized, and stable environments with clear hierarchical guidelines.
- **Entrepreneurial Intent** (`entrepreneurial_intent`) — Inclination towards starting, building, and owning a discrete business or new venture.
- **Career Readiness** (`career_readiness`) — The degree to which an individual feels prepared, equipped, and strategically positioned to enter the professional world.
