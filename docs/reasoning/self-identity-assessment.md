# MyFlow Reasoning Engine — Self Identity

> Pilot category. Each question is mapped to primary/secondary traits, per-option weighted contributions, higher-level dimensions, framework relevance, confidence logic, and downstream contributions.

## New traits introduced

- **Dependability** (`dependability`) — The tendency to be reliable, fulfill obligations, and act consistently over time.

- **Secondary traits:** `reflectiveness`, `risk_taking`, `adaptability`

**Relevant frameworks:**

**Confidence logic:** Requires at least 2 distinct behavioral questions assessing reaction times to validate the primary trait; scaling linearly as independent vs. collaborative decision reports align.

**Contributes to:**

- *Weaknesses:* Flags potential analysis paralysis or reckless action depending on the choice.

- *Learning:* Shows a preference for experiential trial-and-error versus theory-first study.
- *Roadmap:* Suggests implementing either 'bias for action' frameworks or 'measured planning' templates.
- *Podcasts:* Recommends content focused on executive functioning, cognitive biases, or agile execution.
- *Books:* Points to literature on decision sciences, habit formation, or calculated risk.
- *Role Models:* Connects to leaders known for either visionary leaps or methodical strategy.
