# MyFlow Reasoning Engine — Purpose & Ambition

> Each question is mapped to primary/secondary traits, per-option weighted contributions, higher-level dimensions, framework relevance, confidence logic, and downstream contributions.

## New traits introduced

- **Extrinsic Motivation** (`extrinsic_motivation`) — Driven by external rewards, tangible outcomes, status, or external obligations rather than purely inherent satisfaction.
- **Intrinsic Motivation** (`intrinsic_motivation`) — Driven by internal satisfaction, a passion for learning, and the inherent joy of the activity itself.
- **Purposefulness** (`purposefulness`) — The degree to which an individual seeks enduring meaning, societal impact, and a broader sense of direction in life.
- **Long-Term Planning** (`long_term_planning`) — The tendency to project into the future, organizing present actions and behaviors around distant objectives.
