# MyFlow Reasoning Engine — Unlock Your Potential

> Each question is mapped to primary/secondary traits, per-option weighted contributions, higher-level dimensions, framework relevance, confidence logic, and downstream contributions.

## New traits introduced

- **Intrinsic Motivation** (`intrinsic_motivation`) — The drive to engage in activities for their inherent satisfaction and alignment with personal interests rather than external rewards.

- **Secondary traits:** `goal_orientation`, `leadership`, `risk_taking`, `altruism`

### Options

**A. Leading meaningful projects or teams**

| Trait | Weight | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `leadership` | +3 | Projects growth directly into a paradigm of authority, influence, and group direction. |
| `goal_orientation` | +2 | Aims toward taking on greater scope and responsibility within a structured environment. |

**B. Excelling in my chosen career**

| Trait | Weight | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `achievement_drive` | +3 | Focuses on mastery, prestige, and vertical ascent within an established hierarchy or field. |
| `discipline` | +2 | Understand that 'excelling' requires compounding interest via sustained personal industry. |

**C. Starting my own venture or initiative**

| Trait | Weight | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `risk_taking` | +3 | Projects an entrepreneurial trajectory that inherently involves assuming high stakes and ambiguity. |
| `initiative` | +3 | Desires total ownership from 0 to 1, completely circumventing existing structures. |

**D. Making a positive impact on society**

| Trait | Weight | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| `altruism` | +3 | Defines ultimate success by the breadth and depth of utility provided to the broader community. |
| `empathy` | +2 | Maintains a macroscopic view of societal pain points and desires to heal them. |

**Higher-level dimensions:** Long-Term Vision, Motivational Locus

**Relevant frameworks:**
- *Ikigai* — Touches the 'What the world needs' (D) versus 'What you can be paid for/are good at' (B) spheres.
- *SDT* — Reflects basic psychological needs orientation: power/autonomy (C), competence (B), relatedness/impact (D).

**Confidence logic:** Vision-casting statements are highly predictive of latent drives; this provides a robust capstone measure of what 'potential unlocked' actually means to the individual.
