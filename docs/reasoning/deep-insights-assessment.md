# MyFlow Reasoning Engine — Deep Insights About You

> Each question is mapped to primary/secondary traits, per-option weighted contributions, higher-level dimensions, framework relevance, confidence logic, and downstream contributions.

## New traits introduced

- **Introversion** (`introversion`) — Tendency to derive energy from within and prefer solitary or low-stimulation environments.
- **Vulnerability** (`vulnerability`) — Willingness to expose one's authentic feelings, struggles, and uncertainties to others.
- **Perfectionism** (`perfectionism`) — Rejection of mistakes and a desire for flawless outcomes, often hindering timely action.
- **Need for Approval** (`need_for_approval`) — Desire for external validation and recognition to feel secure in one's actions or identity.
